How to Report a Lost or Found Item

Step 1: Create an Account

Sign up or log in to your account to access the report form.

Step 2: Fill the Report Form

Go to the "Report Item" page and provide details about the lost or found item.

Step 3: Submit the Report

Submit the report, and it will be visible in the feed for others to see.

Step 4: Connect with the Owner

Once the owner identifies their item, coordinate with them to return it.
